Description

In this stage, the chatbot employs a clever mathematical method along with the ChatGPT API integration to estimate the user's age and offer tailored content based on this estimation. By engaging the user in a series of questions related to remainders when dividing their age by 3, 5, and 7, the bot aims to not only guess the user's age but also create an intriguing and interactive experience. Subsequently, leveraging the guessed age, the bot provides educational resources or motivational advice that aligns with the user's presumed age group.

Objectives

The primary goal of this stage is to showcase the chatbot's proficiency in logical deduction and its seamless integration with the ChatGPT API to facilitate a more profound and engaging interaction with the user. Specific objectives include:

  1. Engage the user in a series of questions to ascertain the remainders obtained when dividing their age by 3, 5, and 7, fostering an interactive and stimulating dialogue.
  2. Utilize the gathered information to employ a mathematical trick for estimating the user's age, adding an element of intrigue and excitement to the interaction.
  3. Leverage the estimated age to generate personalized recommendations through the ChatGPT API, thereby enhancing user engagement and relevance of the offered content.
